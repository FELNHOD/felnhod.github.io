iACADEMY LAUNCHES FAST-TRACK ACCOUNTANCY PROGRAM

A TWO-YEAR fast track program for Bachelor of Science in Accountancy was launched by iACADEMY.

The program, the first of its kind in the Philippines, was designed to meet the rising demand for highly skilled professionals in the digital age. It was patterned after the successful iACADEMY’s Bachelor of Science in Real Estate Management Fast Track Program, which achieved a 93.75 percent passing rate in the Real Estate Appraisers Licensure Exam in 2022.

The BS Accountancy program is open to 2nd-degree takers and can be completed in just two years, starting from Academic Year 2023-2024. The program is designed to produce a new generation of highly skilled and knowledgeable accountants.

“In light of the rising demand for accountancy professionals in the industry, we find it apt to offer a fast-track program for BS Accountancy in partnership with PricewaterhouseCoopers Philippines, the leading audit and assurance, consulting and tax services provider in the country. Aside from crafting industry-ready programs, we’ve set our sights on offering the best education in the shortest time possible to accelerate the career growth of aspiring accountants and provide them with a competitive edge in the job market,” Vanessa Tanco, President and CEO of iACADEMY, said.

The fast track program is also ideal for those who want to explore new career opportunities in a short amount of time.

Students can pursue a career in accounting without having to spend four to five years in college, which makes it an attractive option for those who are looking to switch careers or want to start working as soon as possible.
